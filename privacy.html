Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Last updated: 03.02.2025

At getAI4all.com („we“, „us“, or „our“), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit and use our website, getAI4all.com. By using the Website, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. What Data We Collect

A. Non-Personal Data

We do not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. However, we automatically collect certain non-personal data when you visit our Website. This data may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Referring pages and time spent
  • Pages viewed on our Website

This information helps us improve our Website and analyze usage.

B. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website uses cookies and similar technologies to track your usage and enhance your user experience. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit our Website. They allow us to better understand your preferences and personalize your experience.

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings, and you may disable them if you prefer. However, please note that some features of the Website may not function properly if you block cookies.

2. Affiliate Links and Cookies

Our Website contains affiliate links that lead to external websites. When you click on one of these affiliate links and make a transaction (e.g., a purchase), we earn a commission. To ensure proper attribution, our affiliate partners may place cookies on your device.

A. Use of Affiliate Cookies

When you click on an affiliate link, a cookie may be placed on your device by the respective affiliate partner (e.g., Amazon, ClickBank, etc.) to track your activity. This allows us to earn a commission if you complete a qualifying action (e.g., make a purchase) on the partner site. The affiliate cookies typically have a limited validity (usually ranging from 24 hours to 30 days, depending on the partner) and are used to track whether you arrived at the partner site via our link and took action.

B. Controlling Affiliate Cookies

You can control the use of cookies by adjusting the settings in your browser. Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may impact the functionality of affiliate links and the attribution of commissions.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our Website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as:

  • The number of visitors
  • The pages viewed
  • Time spent on the Website
  • Referring sites

This data is used to analyze trends, improve the Website, and enhance the user experience. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual users.

A. How to Control Google Analytics Cookies

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Alternatively, you can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies entirely, though this may affect your experience on the Website.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy & Terms.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data, particularly non-personal data such as IP addresses and browser information, for the following purposes:

  • Analyze and improve the usage of our Website
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Optimize affiliate links and commission attribution

We do not collect or use any personal data.

5. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored on secure servers, and we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access. We do not share any personal data with third parties because we do not collect any.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, you do not have specific rights related to personal data. However, if you have questions regarding the non-personal data we collect or need more information, feel free to contact us at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the „Last Updated“ date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need further information, please contact us at:

Conclusion

This Privacy Policy informs you about the use of affiliate cookies, Google Analytics, and other tracking technologies on our Website to enhance the user experience and to earn commissions through affiliate links. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.
