Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) about $A4A Coin

How do I buy the getAI4all coin?

To purchase the getAI4all Coin, please follow these steps:

  • Create a Phantom wallet if you don’t have one yet. Visit the official Phantom website and download the extension.
  • Add Solana (SOL) to your wallet by purchasing it on a cryptocurrency exchange that supports Solana, and transfer the purchased SOL to your Phantom wallet address.
  • Connect your Phantom wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium or Jupiter.
  • Search for “getAI4all” on the DEX.
  • Swap SOL for the getAI4all Coin by entering the desired amount and clicking on "Swap."
  • Confirm the transaction in your Phantom wallet.
  • After a successful swap, the getAI4all Coin will be displayed in your wallet. If you have any questions or encounter any issues, feel free to reach out to us!
What chain is the getAI4all Coin built on?

The getAI4all Coin is built on the Solana blockchain, which is known for its fast transaction speeds and low fees.
